About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an end-to-end AI and Machine Learning project for agricultural production optimization. The system analyzes soil nutrients and environmental parameters, trains crop recommendation models, saves the best model, and exposes prediction through a Flask website.

Objectives

  • Recommend the most suitable crop from seven input values.
  • Improve productivity and farming decision quality.
  • Support sustainable use of fertilizer and water resources.

Technologies

  • Python, Flask, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn
  • HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap-style responsive UI
  • Pickle model loading with model.pkl support
